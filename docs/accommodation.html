Where to Stay

We contacted a few hotels about room blocks, but none of them provided competitive discounts. Since the rate for hotels has a wide range on the Big Island, we decided not to reserve a room block so our guests could feel free to choose where to stay. There are a variety of lodging options close to the venue. Listed below are just a few options we found on Google, and our price information below is as of October 2023, before taxes and fees.

Within walking distance:

  • Royal Kona Resort

    Address: 75-5852 Ali’i Dr, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740

We think this one is the best value for what it is. Standard room rates started at $239/night.

  • Courtyard by Marriott King Kamehameha’s Kona Beach Hotel

    Address: 75-5660 Palani Rd, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740

This hotel is more expensive, with rates around $400/night. They do have a little beach and it’s also a Marriott property if you have points with the brand.

10-15 minute drive away:

  • Kona Coast Resort

    Address: 78-6842 Ali‘i Dr, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740

Condo units with full kitchens. ~$260/night for a 2 bed/2 bath. We notice somehow the tax and fee charge for this resort is significantly less than the other options on hotels.com.

  • Kanaloa at Kona by Outrigger

    Address: 78-261 Manukai St, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740

Waterfront condos with full kitchens. $269/night for a 2 bed/2 bath.

  • Outrigger Kona Resort & Spa

    Address: 78-128 Ehukai St, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740

Room rates are around $300 - $400. They have a nice pool.

If you prefer to have a full kitchen during your stay, we also recommend looking into airbnb or vrbo. Please don’t hesitate to reach out to us if you have any questions.
